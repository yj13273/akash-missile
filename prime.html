Akash Prime

Refined Design for All-Weather Performance

Overview

Akash Prime is a further improved version of Akash-1S, built to perform better in cold and high-altitude regions. It features optimized software and a more reliable fuse.

Key Features

  • Optimized seeker and fuse for cold conditions
  • Improved tracking and precision
  • Reliable operation in sub-zero environments
  • Tested successfully in 2021

Applications

Akash Prime is well-suited for deployment in regions like Ladakh and the Northeast, enhancing India's multi-layered air defense system.
