Akash-NG

Next-Generation Missile with Active Radar Seeker

Overview

Akash-NG (Next Generation) is the most advanced variant of the Akash family, with canisterized launch, active radar seeker, and a range of 70 km.

Next-Gen Capabilities

  • Range: ~70 km
  • Seeker: Active RF seeker
  • Launcher: Canister-based cold launch
  • Faster reaction time
  • Lightweight launcher and improved mobility

Deployment Goals

Intended to replace older variants for better readiness and survivability in modern warfare. Ideal for both high-threat air defense zones and rapid deployment.
