Akash-1S

Enhanced Accuracy with Indigenous Seeker

Overview

Akash-1S is an upgraded version of Akash Mk-I, featuring an indigenous seeker for terminal guidance, offering improved accuracy and electronic countermeasure resistance.

Improvements Over Mk-I

  • Seeker-based terminal guidance
  • Better accuracy against fast-moving targets
  • Improved ECM immunity
  • Enhanced target acquisition via upgraded radar

Trials

Akash-1S was tested in 2019 at the Integrated Test Range in Odisha and successfully intercepted aerial targets under various conditions.
